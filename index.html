Everything You Wanted To Know About Whoopee Cushions! (But Were Afraid To Ask!)

What is it that makes us laugh at people who unwittingly sit on whoopee cushions? Is it the sound? is it accusing them of breaking wind? Or is it just the fact that they didn't know it was there?

Whoopee cushions have been around for several decades and come in various sizes and colors. There are even electronic cushions that operate via a remote control. Some cushions are large enough for TWO people to jump on!

Yes, Whoopee cushions have been around for many years, and come in a lot of different variations, too. but it's the same old wind-breaking sounds that seem to make us laugh every time!
